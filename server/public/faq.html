Frequently Asked Questions.

How much do I get charged?

  • This service is currently provided at no charge.

  • When we introduce fees, we intend the fee structure to be on the basis of 'shared gain': we will get paid when you also make more money, so for example a charge per class that is small relative to the fees you charge your clients.

How do you keep my data secure?

  • We use leading industry providers for all your sensitive data - we do not store any data relating to your passwords or your customers card data.

  • Your password is maintained by Facebook, who have industry leading security. We use your Facebook ID to verify your identity, and only this ID can be used to access your account data. All data transmitted over the internet is encrypted with standard industry prototcols (https).

  • All sensitive card data is processed by Stripe, who are a PCI Level 1 Service Provider, the most stringent level of certification available. You can confirm Stripe's certification in Visa's registry of service providers.

  • All Facebook and Stripe trademarks are ackowledged and copyright of their respective organisations.

What hardware do I need?

  • Our application works on most modern phones, tablets, or computers.

  • We currently test on Apple iOS 13 on iPhone and iPad, PCs running Windows 10 and Edge 89, or Chrome 89, Apple Mac running MacOS Siera (10.12) or Chrome 89. Technically, we require the use of a modern browser that supports HTML5. Almost any phone, tablet, or PC/laptop sold in the past couple of years will support this.

What if I decide I don't like your service and want to leave?

  • Obviously we hope this won't happen. Our objective is to provide the best way for Box operators to provide online experiences, bar none.

  • Nevertheless we realise you still may choose another provider. You can leave at any time with no exit charges.

How do I get help if I have a problem?

  • Get in touch with us - we run an email help line at ultrabox.support@gmail.com.

  • Unfortunately we can't provide a phone number. Please don't take this as a sign of disinterest, but if we ran a help-line we simply would not be able to offer you the same pricing. Once we get your mail, then if we need to we are happy to get on the phone.
